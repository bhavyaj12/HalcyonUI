Avatar

Avatars are used to show a user's profile picture on a website.

Image Avatar

Avatars with image are available in 5 different sizes - extra small (xs), small (sm), medium (md), large (lg) and extra large (xl). To use the avatar in your site, add the base class avatar and add any one of the following classes for the different sizes - avatar-xs, avatar-sm, avatar-md, avatar-lg, and avatar-xl.
